Why are safe restrooms or Refuges so important?

Using the restroom is an everyday activity that can be difficult for tansgender, intersex, and gender nonconforming individuals.

According to the US Trans Survey:

  • 59% of respondents avoided using a restroom for fear of confrontation
  • 31% avoided eating or drinking while out to avoid having to use the restroom
  • 24% had their presence in a restroom questioned
  • 12% had been harassed, attacked, or sexually assaulted in a restroom
  • 9% had been denied restroom access
  • 8% had developed a kidney issue or urinary tract infection from avoiding bathroom usage
